Women In Soil Ecology

Connecting Women Scientists Through Soil and Ecology


Mission

Our network is aimed at connecting women scientists to build a strong community and further the fight for equity of all those identifying as womxn in science. We aim to establish connections among senior and junior ecologists through meetups, workshops, and peer-to-peer mentorship.

Email List

Passionate about women's mentorship in science? Passionate about soil? We are too! If you are a woman soil scientist and want to join our group, fill out the Google form to be added to the email list. All career stages welcome! Sign up here
