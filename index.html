Women In Soil Ecology

Connecting Women Through Soils



Mission

Our network is aimed at connecting women scientists to build a strong community and further the fight for equity of all those identifying as womxn in science. We aim to establish connections among senior and junior ecologists through meetups, workshops, and peer-to-peer mentorship.
